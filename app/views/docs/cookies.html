Cookies

Cookies are small files put onto your device when you visit and browse a website. We use cookies to make the service work and collect information about how you use our service. You will normally see a message on the site before we store a cookie on your computer.

Cookies aren't used to identify you. You can delete cookies from your device. Find out how to manage cookies.

The cookies we use

Settings cookies

These cookies do things like remember your preferences and the choices you make, to personalise your experience of using the service. You may see a banner when you visit the service inviting you to accept cookies or review your settings. We’ll set cookies so that your computer knows you’ve seen it and not to show it again, and also to store your settings.

Settings cookies
Cookie name What it does Expires
cookie_consent Saves your cookie consent settings 1 year
cookies_preferences_set Lets us know that you’ve saved your cookie consent settings 1 year

Essential cookies

Essential cookies keep your information secure while you use the service. We do not need to ask permission to use them.

Essential cookies
Cookie name What it does Expires
Apache This cookie is used for directing the users to the same server Until you close your browser
JESSIONID Used to maintain users' state across page requests Until you close your browser
ROUTEID This cookie is used for directing the users to the same server Until you close your browser

Google Analytics cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect data about how you use the service. This information helps us to improve our service.

Google Analytics stores anonymised information about:

  • how you got to the service
  • the pages you visit on the service and how long you spend on them
  • what you click on while you’re visiting the site
Google Analytics cookies
Cookie name What it does Expires
_ga Helps us count how many people visit the service by tracking if you’ve visited before 2 years
_gid Helps us count how many people visit GOV.UK by tracking if you’ve visited before 1 day
_gat Manages the rate at which page view requests are made 1 minute

Google doesn’t collect or store your personal information, for example your name or address, so this information can’t be used to identify who you are.

We don’t allow Google to use or share our analytics data.

Third-party cookies

Our feedback survey

We use Qualtrics to collect feedback comments. We do this to help make sure the service is meeting the needs of its users and to help us make improvements. See the Qualtrics privacy statement for more information.
