Cookies

Cookies are small files put onto your device when you visit and browse a website. We use cookies to make the service work and collect information about how you use our service.

Cookies aren't used to identify you.

Find out how to manage cookies.

Essential cookies

Essential cookies keep your information secure while you use the service. We do not need to ask permission to use them.

Analytics cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect data about how you use the service. This information helps us to improve our service.

Google is not allowed to use or share our analytics data with anyone.

Google Analytics stores anonymised information about:

  • how you got to the service
  • the pages you visit on the service and how long you spend on them
  • any errors you see while using the service
