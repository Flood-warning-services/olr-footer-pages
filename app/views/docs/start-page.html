Sign up for flood warnings

Sign up to get warnings in England by phone, email or text message if your home or business is at risk of flooding. The service is free.

You’ll need to provide:

  • the address you want to get flood warnings for
  • your name
  • an email address
  • contact details to get flood warnings day or night

Sign in to your account if you want to update your details or cancel your account.

Sign up for flood warnings in Scotland or flood warnings in Wales. You can also check the risk of flooding in Northern Ireland.

Other ways to register

If you’re in England, Scotland or Wales you can register, update your details or cancel your account by calling Floodline.

Floodline

  • Telephone: 0345 988 1188
  • Textphone: 0345 602 6340
  • 24 hour service
  • Find out about call charges

Warnings for multiple locations

If you’re in England or Wales, you can register to the Targeted Flood Warning Service. This service provides warnings if your business or organisation has multiple locations.

The service is free for not-for-profit and public sector organisations, but there is a charge of £4,700 a year for other organisations.

To register for the Targeted Flood Warning Service, call the National Customer Contact Centre.

National Customer Contact Centre

  • Telephone: 03708 506 506
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm
  • Find out about call charges
